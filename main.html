Project Overview

Our project was a laser communication system that had two main components: A receiver and a transmitter.

The transmitter's purpose was two main things: Track the receiver's IR LEDs, and encode data at a goal transmission rate of 10 kBits/sec.
The receiver had two of its own goals: Read if a laser is hitting it, and process whatever data the laser may be sending. Ultimately, this system allows us to move the reciever anywhere within a 30 foot range, and the laser will be able to track it, and beam it data using our laser.
