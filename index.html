Optical laser communication between a ground station and a moving object.

Concept

We build and operate a laser-based optical communication link that maintains continuous data flow between a fixed ground station and a moving target. Precision pointing, adaptive beam control, and deterministic software keep latency predictable and throughput stable in dynamic conditions.

Disciplines
Mechanical: gimbal design, vibration isolation, and beam stabilization to hold alignment under motion.
